About the Founder & Our Mission

The vision for The Heritage Letters was born out of a profound personal realization.

Both of Mark's parents, as well as his maternal and paternal grandparents, passed away before modern digital tools were available to easily record and preserve their life stories. Realizing that their firsthand wisdom, vibrant memories, and hard-earned life lessons were lost to time, he became determined to ensure no other family has to experience that same silence.

The Heritage Letters was created as a bridge across time—empowering people to preserve and transmit their heritage, their authentic stories, and their legacy to future generations.

Meet Mark Henry Saft

Mark Henry Saft has spent a lifetime bridging cultures and building connections. Originally from the United States, Mark has spent the last three decades living and working across Asia—including 23 years in Japan, time in Thailand, and his current home in Hong Kong.

Professionally, he serves as the APAC Head of Business Development for AIDAv2.com. His extensive global experience and background in scaling business operations have shown him the universal human need for connection. He is now utilizing those same strategic and technological principles to build a platform where the timeless wisdom of our elders can be safely housed and celebrated for decades to come.
